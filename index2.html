# Welcome to KNEPP We're a KTH photo club that loves all new and old methods of creating pictures. We always welcome new members * [Get Started](#first)
![](images/intro2.jpg)
## We organize meetings with cool photographers There's tons of super smart photographers in Stockholm with whom we organize talks, workshops and exhibitions. We learn a lot and it's ton's of fun. If you'd like to meet up with our members, let us know! * [Learn More](#)
![](images/photographer.jpg)
## We visit cool exhibitions In Stockholm we have very cool museums with good pictures. We love going there, getting a private tour with super cool guides and learning tons about what makes a good picture. * [Learn More](#)
![](images/museum.jpg)
## We do photo walks Nothing beats getting into field and making real pictures of real things. We go together and hunt for decisive moments. * [Learn More](#)
![](images/walk.jpg)
## We have our own darkoom Shooting film is great way to get closer to your camera and technology and there's nothing better than making your own print. We have tons of great equipment and organize courses.
